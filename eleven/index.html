Our Big Idea

‍The first AI-powered rehearsal partner.

More than a line-learning tool, it reacts at near-human speed to provide a true reader-like experience when actors have no other choice.

Linus restores the natural flow of live rehearsal—no more fumbling with play/pause buttons or rigid playback. Unlike static apps, Linus listens, reacts, and moves with the actor. It tracks rhythm, intent, and energy of the scene.

If an actor paraphrases, skips a word, or shifts pacing, Linus responds—keeping momentum alive. It takes direction instantly; adjusting tone, speed, and delivery based on performance notes.

Who we are

Some ideas don’t belong to just one person. They hover, waiting for the right people to bring them to life. Linus was one of those ideas.

Callan Colley

At 18, Callan led R&D for an accounting firm while pursuing a double degree in Geology & Finance. A deeper calling brought him to NIDA, where he saw an industry in flux—self-taping was standard, yet actors weren’t trained for it.

So, Callan created Actors Kit, providing self-taping solutions globally.

Alfred Kouris

Alfred excelled in both tech and the arts. By 19, he built a #1 Education App on the Australian App Store and scaled a digital agency while studying Commerce & Theatre at Monash. At 21, he trained as an actor at NIDA.

These two NIDA Acting alumni, barely acquaintances, met by chance in a sauna, geeking out over business, tech, and the industry. They quickly realised they were both working on the same idea.
