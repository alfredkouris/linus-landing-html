Linus Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 22/07/2025

Your privacy matters to us. This policy outlines what we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle your information. We want you to be able to focus on your performance knowing your data is protected.

1. Information We Collect

Email Address

We collect your email when you sign up for Linus. We use it to manage your account, communicate with you (like sending updates or support responses), and personalise your experience.

Device Information

We collect basic device data (such as device type and operating system version) to ensure Linus works properly on your phone or tablet.

Optional Interaction Data

We collect anonymous data about how you interact with Linus's AI (for example, timing of cues or playback). We use this optional data to improve Linus's performance and make your rehearsal partner smarter. This data does not include your actual script content or personal details. You can opt out of this at any time via the Linus app.

No Script Content Storage

We do not collect or store the scripts or scene content you upload or rehearse. Your creative work stays private to you and on your device.

Payment Information

We do not handle your payment details directly. Purchases and subscriptions are processed securely through third-party platforms (such as the Apple App or Google Play store). Linus itself never sees or stores your card or payment details.

2. Infrastructure and Analytics

Supabase

Linus uses Supabase for backend infrastructure, user authentication, and analytics. This helps us monitor performance (like crash reports), maintain reliability, and deploy new features seamlessly.

Analytics SDKs & Usage Data

We may use additional tools (such as Meta SDK, Google Analytics for Firebase, or similar SDKs) to understand how Linus is used. This data includes in-app behaviors, crash logs, feature usage, and anonymized interaction patterns. These tools help us improve the experience and make Linus smarter and more useful over time.

This may include first-party and third-party cookies or SDK tracking, depending on the platform or version of Linus you're using.

All collected data is either anonymized or aggregated. We do not sell this data, and we use it exclusively to improve performance, fix bugs, and enhance your rehearsal experience.

3. Push Notifications and Marketing

Linus may occasionally send you push notifications or emails to help you get the most out of the app (e.g., new features, rehearsal tips, actor-related updates). We only do this if you've opted in.

You can opt out at any time via in-app settings or the unsubscribe link in our emails.

We also track basic email engagement (such as open rates or clicks) using pixels, so we can understand what communication is helpful. This helps us keep messages relevant, and not spammy.

4. Voice Technology and AI Ethics

All character voices in Linus are AI-generated clones of real actors' voices, used with their full consent. Each actor is compensated and retains an interest in how their voice is used.

These voices are licensed for use only within the Linus app for personal rehearsals only. They are not to be used commercially or externally.

We do not sell, share, or rent these voice models to anyone else. They exist strictly to help you rehearse in Linus, and we protect them accordingly.

When we use third-party AI services to understand your scripts and deliver responses (for example, for voice processing via ElevenLabs or text understanding via cloud AI providers), we only send the minimum data needed for the feature. These providers are obligated to handle your data securely and only for providing the experience of Linus.

5. Device Access and Permissions

To provide core functionality, Linus may request access to certain features on your device:

  • Microphone: for recording your lines or enabling voice interaction.
  • Storage: to upload scripts, save audio or selftape recordings.
  • Camera: only if you use the self-tape feature.

We only request access when needed. You can manage permissions in your device settings. Disabling access may limit some features.

6. Cookies and Website Tracking

While our app doesn't use traditional browser cookies, we may use tracking technologies to better understand user journey within the app, on our website or in regards to our emails to better understand usage and improve our services.

These include:

  • Google Analytics on our website to track visits and improve site performance
  • Email pixels to understand whether our messages are useful

These tools help us understand things like:

  • Which features or pages are most useful
  • How users interact with the app
  • Whether an email was opened or clicked through

This data is anonymised or aggregated. We do not sell this data.

If you prefer not to be tracked, you can disable cookies in your browser or opt out using tools provided by platforms like Google or Meta.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosures

We respect your privacy and handle your data with care. We do not sell your personal data and will only share limited data in these cases:

  • With Service Providers: Like our cloud partners, to help us provide the Linus service. They only get what they need and are contractually required to protect your data.
  • If Required by Law: Such as in response to a legal request, court order, or to protect safety or legal rights.
  • If Linus is Acquired: Your data may transfer to the new owner. If this happens, we'll ensure your data is still protected, or ask for your consent if required.
  • If You Violate Terms: If someone uses Linus for unlawful activity (like uploading copyrighted material), we may act to protect other users and our community. Which could include removing the content or disabling the user's account, in accordance with our Terms of Service.

8. Data Storage and Retention

We store your data as long as needed to:

  • Operate Linus
  • Provide support
  • Comply with legal obligations

If you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your data. Some info may persist briefly in backups.

Your data may be stored in secure servers located in countries such as the U.S., Europe, or Australia. Wherever it's stored, we apply the same security standards and comply with data transfer regulations (e.g. GDPR Standard Contractual Clauses).

9. Children's Privacy

Linus is designed for users 13 and older. Users between 13–17 must use Linus with parental consent or supervision.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 13. If we discover we have, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has used Linus, please contact us immediately.

10. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct or update info
  • Delete your account
  • Withdraw consent (e.g., for optional tracking)
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Object to certain uses
  • Lodge a complaint with your country's data authority

You can make many changes in the app. For other requests, email us at support@getlinus.app. We will respond as required by law and never penalise you for exercising your rights.

11. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your data. Linus uses industry-standard security measures to safeguard your information:

Encrypted data transfers

Data you provide to Linus is transmitted over secure (encrypted) connections.

Secure servers and backups

We store data in secure cloud environments that implement strong security practices (like firewalls, encryption at rest, and regular security audits).

Role-based access control

Our team members are trained on privacy and security, and only authorised personnel can access personal data, and even then, only what they need to do their jobs.

Ongoing security monitoring

No system is 100% secure, but if there's ever a breach that affects your data, we'll notify you and the relevant authorities quickly, as required.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy as Linus evolves or legal standards change. If we make major changes:

  • We'll post the updated policy here
  • We'll notify you via email or in-app

Continued use of Linus means you agree to the updated terms. If you disagree, you may stop using Linus and delete your account.
